The primary entry point for all data hydration.
*   **Search Protocol:** Enter any city, village, or landmark.

*   **Tactical Radar:** Anchored at the absolute bottom of the cockpit for high-fidelity regional monitoring.

## 3. The SkyID Ecosystem & SkyPoints
Vyamir features a proprietary gamified economy to reward user engagement.
*   **The Vault:** Perform Peer-to-Peer (P2P) transfers of SkyPoints to other Vyamir citizens.
*   **Referral Network:** Generate a unique link from your Vault to earn specialized growth bonuses.

## 4. Administrative Governance
